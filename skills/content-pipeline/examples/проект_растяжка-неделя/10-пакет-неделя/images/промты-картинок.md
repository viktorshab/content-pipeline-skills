## Промты

**Пост (4:5).**
Warm editorial fitness photo, vertical 4:5. A calm adult in comfortable activewear sitting on a yoga mat in a sunlit minimal studio, gently reaching toward the floor in a forward fold, relaxed face — no strain, no pain. Soft natural light, muted warm palette (beige, terracotta), shallow depth of field. Clear space at the top for a short headline. Photorealistic, supportive mood, not aggressive sport. _(Если в кадре своё лицо — identity-lock по фото-референсу.)_

**Тред (16:9).**
Wide horizontal 16:9, clean educational-style photo. Same warm minimal studio. Focus on the hips and lower-back area of a person in a forward fold, a soft subtle highlight on the pelvis / lower-back zone (where the real restriction sits), legs relaxed. Calm mood, muted warm palette, room on the left for text. Photorealistic, no text rendered in the image.

**Reels (9:16).**
Vertical 9:16, talking-head frame. Same person, face large and centered, warm even lighting, looking directly into camera with a calm reassuring expression. Soft minimal studio background, warm palette. Keep the lower third clear for a caption. Photorealistic. _(Лицо точь-в-точь по фото-референсу.)_

**Telegram (16:9).**
Wide horizontal 16:9, cozy and personal. A warm slightly-overhead shot of a yoga mat with a cup of tea beside it, soft morning light through a window — the "for our people" calm Telegram mood. Muted warm tones, people optional, space for a headline. Photorealistic, inviting.

## Честная пометка по размерам
Лента/карусель — **4:5**, сторис/Reels — **9:16**, тред/Telegram — **16:9**. У встроенного генератора Codex вертикаль = 2:3 (1024×1536); ровно 4:5 даёт кроп или Replicate `aspect_ratio:"4:5"` (см. `references/06-модели-возможности.md` в `carousel-builder`). **Рендер — руками:** сборщик отдаёт готовые промты + объём/цену, кнопку «сгенерировать» нажимает человек.
