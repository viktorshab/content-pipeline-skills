# 5 визуальных стилей карусели - DNA (дословный перенос Vibe)

> Каждый стиль = визуальная ДНК (`promptDNA`) + тон копирайта. `promptDNA` подставляется в image-промт через `STYLE: {…}`. Тон уходит в копирайтер (см. `01-text-phase.md`).

---

## EXPERT - профессиональный кейноут (дефолт для экспертного/бизнес-контента)

```
Instagram carousel slide.
LIGHT clean background: white (#FFFFFF), soft gray (#F5F5F5), or light cream (#FAFAF0).
Professional clean layout like a tech keynote presentation or Apple product page.
Headline in bold modern sans-serif font (Inter, SF Pro, or Montserrat style), black or dark navy text.
Text in clean rounded cards with white background and subtle drop shadow (4px blur, 10% opacity).
One accent color used SPARINGLY for highlights, underlines, and icon tints - choose based on topic.
Subtle geometric grid lines or thin hairline dividers for structure.
Professional studio lighting: soft, even, no harsh shadows.
Premium minimalist feel - every element has breathing room.
IMPORTANT: Background is LIGHT. Text is DARK on light background. High contrast.
Subtle depth details: cards have 12px rounded corners with micro-shadow (4px blur, 8% black). Thin accent-colored line (2px) as section separator. Background has barely visible radial gradient from center (white to #F0F0F0) for dimension. Icons are thin line-style (1.5px stroke), not filled solid.
```
Тон: профессиональный, авторитетный, с данными и фактами.

## PHOTOREALISTIC - как настоящее фото

```
Instagram carousel slide.
LIGHT or contextual background - determined by the real-world scene (natural daylight preferred).
PHOTOREALISTIC image quality - indistinguishable from a real photograph taken by a professional photographer.
Shot on a high-end DSLR camera (Canon EOS R5 or Sony A7R V) with a prime lens (85mm f/1.4 or 50mm f/1.2).
Natural lighting: golden hour sunlight, soft window light, or professional studio strobes with diffusion.
Shallow depth of field with creamy bokeh on background. Sharp focus on the main subject.
Realistic skin textures, fabric weaves, material surfaces - every detail is physically accurate.
Real-world environments: actual rooms, streets, offices, cafes, nature - NOT CGI or 3D renders.
Color grading like a professional photo: natural tones, accurate white balance, subtle film grain.
Composition follows rule of thirds. Natural poses and expressions if people are present.
NO illustration, NO cartoon, NO 3D render, NO flat design, NO vector art - ONLY photographic realism.
Camera details: visible lens characteristics - natural chromatic aberration at edges, subtle vignetting (5-10% darker corners), authentic sensor noise at ISO 400-800. Depth: background 3-5 stops out of focus with circular bokeh highlights. Color: slight warm shift (5600K), lifted blacks in shadows.
```
Тон: детализированный, реалистичный, как профессиональная фотография.

## LIFESTYLE - мягкий пастельный эстетик

```
Instagram carousel slide.
LIGHT warm pastel gradient background: peach-to-rose (#FFE5D9 to #FFCAD4), lavender-to-cream (#E8D5F5 to #FFF5E6), or mint-to-sky (#D4F0E7 to #D4E6F1).
Soft golden hour lighting with dreamy bokeh highlights in the background.
Text in semi-transparent frosted glass card (glassmorphism: white 30% opacity, 12px blur, 1px white border).
Delicate serif font for headline (Playfair Display or Cormorant style), thin sans-serif for body.
Organic shapes: soft blobs, flower petals, or natural textures as subtle accents.
Warm pink, peach, or lavender tint on all elements. Soft and dreamy overall mood.
Like an Instagram aesthetic page or Pinterest mood board.
Dreamy details: frosted glass cards (backdrop-filter: blur 12px, white 30% opacity, 1px white border). Soft bokeh circles (3-5 large, pastel-tinted) in background. Organic shapes: one asymmetric soft blob in accent color behind text area. Gold or pearl micro-particles scattered (8-12 tiny dots).
```
Тон: мягкий, вдохновляющий, поэтичный (первое лицо уместно - «мой опыт», «я поняла»).

## PRODUCT - товар-герой (e-commerce)

```
Instagram carousel slide.
LIGHT clean e-commerce background: white (#FFFFFF), soft gray (#F5F5F5), or light cream (#FAFAF0).
Product is the HERO - large, centered, dominating 50–60% of the frame with studio product photography quality.
Professional product lighting: soft key light, subtle rim light, clean drop shadow beneath the product.
Feature callouts and spec badges as clean design elements: thin lines pointing to product details, small rounded badges with short labels.
Headline in bold modern sans-serif (Inter, SF Pro, or Montserrat style) carrying the product name or key benefit. Black or dark navy text on light background.
Card-based layouts with soft drop shadows (4px blur, 10% opacity) and 12px rounded corners.
One accent color used SPARINGLY for highlights, price badges, and icon tints - chosen based on product category.
Premium catalog feel - trustworthy, clear, conversion-focused. Like Apple product page or high-end e-commerce listing.
IMPORTANT: Background is LIGHT and CLEAN. No cluttered environment. Product and text are both fully visible.
```
Тон: benefit-driven, конкретный, товар-центричный - характеристики, выгоды, доказательства.

## CUSTOM - свободный стиль (когда пользователь задаёт свой)

```
Instagram carousel slide. Custom style: {описание пользователя}. Clean professional layout with high contrast text. Topic-relevant visual elements. Background mood is dictated by the literal topic content: if the topic mentions a place, era, season, or scene - reproduce THAT scene faithfully. Do NOT add cosmic, space, galactic, starfield, or generic "epic" backgrounds unless the topic itself is literally about space/astronomy.
```
Если своего описания нет - «Modern scroll-stopping design», фон по литеральному смыслу темы (лето 2003 Одесса → тёплый пляж с зерном плёнки, НЕ космос; AI-стартап → чистый техно, НЕ космос; маркетинг-советы → яркий редакторский флэт-дизайн, НЕ космос). Запрещённые фоны по умолчанию: cosmic, space, galactic, starfield, planetary.
Тон: нейтральный, адаптивный.

---

**Сквозное правило всех стилей:** фон диктуется ЛИТЕРАЛЬНЫМ смыслом темы. Никогда не ставить космос / галактику / звёздное небо по умолчанию, если тема буквально не про космос/астрономию.
